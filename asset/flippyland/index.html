Project

Flippyland

Casual Block-rolling mobile game
Google Play

About

Flippyland is a puzzle game launched on Google Play and Apple App Store. It is developed using Unity, FireBase, Google Play Games and Apple Game Center. The only goal of the game is to flip the block into position.

Prototype

The early draft of the game aims at testing the game play. During the development process, it was found that perspective views are not as easy as orthographics views when playing. Also, free rotation of camera and swiping screens was a touble for players to control. For better game play experience, I adopted buttons control finally.

Google Ads

A Google Ads campaign has been used to promote Flippyland . After some market research, the campaign was run in South East Asia countries such as India, Phillipine and Malaysia. In Google Ads's report, most of the engaged users were from India.

Firebase

With Firebase analytics, Flippland is able to track user activities in game. These data will be used to analysis users' behavior and adjust our content accordingly.
