VR Monopoly

Combination of motion capture and VR first person monopoly game

About

VR monopoly is a VR game that takes body motion as input to control the avatar of the monopoly game. Our team utilized Kinect to capture body motion and hand guesture as pass along those data to Unity, which used to develop the monopoly game. The screen image was output to VR device for realistic experiences as if the player is inside a monopoly game.

Multiplayer

As monopoly game has to be multiplayer game, we try to implement it to LAN multiplayer rather than local multiplayer. The netcode we used is Unity native UNET. In UNET framework, each player is connected to a special client that serves as a host. Players' data are sent to the host are propagate to every client. The main job of our netcode is to synchronize those data to keep everyone experiencing the same progress.

Walking detection

In walking motion detection, Kinect detected the motion of left knee first. When the left knee was lifted, it started to detect the motion of right knee. If the left knee and right knee were lifted in a specific interval, Kinect will determine that the walking motion was performed. The motion of lifting left knee and right knee were detected by calculating the different positions of left knee and right knee. When the differences were greater than a specific value, lifting of left or right knee was detected.
